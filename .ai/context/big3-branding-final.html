05
Voice & Tone
Big3 speaks like a smart friend who happens to understand astrology — never mystical, never condescending. We translate cosmic complexity into clarity.

Clear, not mystical

We explain what's happening in the sky in plain language. No jargon without context.

"Saturn is pressing on your Moon today. Emotions might feel heavier than usual — that's normal."

Not this

Avoid fortune-teller energy, vague promises, or overly dramatic cosmic language.

"The celestial energies are aligning to transform your spiritual essence during this powerful lunation portal."

Confident, not bossy

We present data and let people decide. The TII tells you the weather — you decide what to wear.

"TII 84° — Intense day. Good for deep work, less ideal for difficult conversations."

Not this

We never tell people what to do or make fear-based predictions.

"WARNING: Do NOT sign contracts today! Mercury retrograde will destroy your plans!"
